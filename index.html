Share your screen in seconds

BeamRoom lets a host iPhone mirror its screen to nearby devices running BeamRoom. On the host, open the Share tab and create a room, then choose BeamRoom from Control Centre’s screen recording button. The host screen appears on connected viewers.

Host on one device, watch nearby

Use an iPhone as the host and watch from other nearby devices running BeamRoom. BeamRoom is built for simple, same-room sharing between a host and a small number of devices in the area, rather than large remote audiences.

Local-only, peer-to-peer streaming

BeamRoom focuses on nearby sharing:

  • Devices discover each other over Wi-Fi and Apple’s peer-to-peer technologies.
  • Screen video and optional microphone audio flow directly between devices on the local network.
  • There are no BeamRoom-run servers relaying or storing your stream.

ReplayKit capture and adaptive video

BeamRoom uses Apple’s ReplayKit to capture the host screen and encode it on-device before sending it to viewers. Video adapts to network conditions, aiming to keep the stream smooth even when Wi-Fi gets busy.

Made for iOS 26

BeamRoom is designed for iOS 26 and later, using modern Apple frameworks including ReplayKit and Network.framework. There are no third-party analytics SDKs and no advertising. No account is required.
